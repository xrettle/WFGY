## Overview

Frontier Challenges is the high-difficulty challenge area under **WFGY 5.0 Polaris Protocol**.

This area is reserved for public-safe reasoning tracks, mathematical challenge materials, external-review packages, and long-term frontier experiments.

The first flagship track is the **Millennium Problems Review Track**.

More challenge tracks may be added later.
