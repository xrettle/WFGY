## Research status notice

All PDFs listed here are exploratory research documents authored by PSBigBig.

Unless a specific paper explicitly states otherwise, these materials have not undergone formal academic peer review.
They are shared as open research artifacts for independent reading, critique, and replication attempts.

No claim is made on this page that any document establishes new physical laws, overturns scientific consensus, or provides experimentally confirmed results.
If a paper proposes experiments, the correct interpretation is that they are proposed or preliminary until independently replicated.
