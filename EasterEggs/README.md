# WFGY Easter Eggs

Small experimental releases from the WFGY line.

These are not full product launches. They are early playable pieces, prompts, add ons, and hidden mechanisms released before larger systems go online.

Each Easter Egg is meant to be simple to try, easy to copy, and useful on its own.

## Available Easter Eggs

| Easter Egg | Description |
|---|---|
| [Cite First Verification](./CiteFirstVerification/) | Makes AI expose confidence before it sounds certain. |

## What are these?

WFGY Easter Eggs are small pieces released before the main work is ready.

Some are tiny mechanisms.
Some are prompts.
Some are experimental reasoning layers.
Some are previews of larger ideas that may appear in future WFGY systems.

They are shared early so builders can play with them, test them, break them, and understand the direction before the next major release arrives.

## Current signal

The first Easter Egg is Cite First Verification.

It adds compact confidence scores to AI answers and makes uncertainty harder to hide.
